About Us

Atlantic Systems Group, Inc. (ASG), is a premier information security solutions provider in North America. Dedicated to providing innovative and integrated solutions, ASG effectively manages our client’s information security risks. We align each client’s business initiatives with effective and proven security best practices, and we combine our deep industry expertise with extensive security knowledge while utilizing structured industry frameworks along with our own methodologies to ensure successful engagements. ASG is a leader in offering end-to-end information security solutions by building and managing security programs across the entire enterprise. We do this with a suite of offerings that span from Strategic Consulting Services, where we help customers meet security compliance requirements; to the more “hands-on” Execution or Deployment Services, which include services like Design and Engineering and Security Assessment; to Operational Services such as Support and Training. Technology has always been fundamental to ASG. We are aligned with the premier information security product vendors and have direct access to the most current products and product information. The Atlantic Systems Group technology competencies span 60 product lines. Our keen focus on our customer’s needs keeps us vigilant in our quest for constant training and certifications so that we can best meet your requirements. Our unparalleled team of consultants are certified in all of the major information security technologies; and typically have more than seven years of direct industry experience. Atlantic Systems Group represents such a breadth of technologies because we know that only a technology-agnostic company can recommend the right solution for you every time. ASG offers integrated security solutions, deliver doorway to desktop solutions, including information security, physical security, and secure application development. ASG has a client base across the nation. We serve clients in the government, defense, telecommunications, commercial, health care and retail markets. Based outside Cape Canaveral Air Force Station in Florida with satellite offices in Washington, DC, ASG currently has sales, consulting and training offices throughout the Eastern and Mid-Western regions of the United States.  

Our People

Atlantic Systems Group, Inc. (ASG), is one of the premier information security solutions provider in North America. We lead the market because of the highly skilled people we employ. Our security engineers and consultants are diverse, dynamic and talented. They average more than a decade of experience in the information security space and represent more than 60 security related technologies, holding certifications in nearly all of them. Our team also possesses CISSP and SANS GIAC certifications. With direct project and program management experience, our experts service the most complex, security centric projects for our small business, large enterprise and government clients. They are thought-leaders in Security Compliance, Vulnerability Assessments, Certification and Accreditation, and Identity and Access Management.
